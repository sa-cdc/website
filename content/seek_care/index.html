Seeking Dental Care

SA-CDC was established in 1986 to provide free dental care for low income and homeless Bexar County adults. Before you schedule a visit, please fill out the form on this page to determine eligibility.

All clients must be age 18 or older and reside in San Antonio/Bexar County. Care is provided without regard for race, creed, or gender.
