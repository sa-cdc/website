Partnering Organizations

SAMMinistries

SAMMinistries is an interfaith ministry whose mission is to help the homeless and those at risk of becoming homeless attain self-sufficiency by offering, with dignity and compassion, shelter, housing, and services. SAMMinistries also provides volunteers opportunities to be renewed and fulfilled as they serve.

WellMed

WellMed's mission is to change the face of healthcare delivery for seniors. They have been recognized as a leader in preventive care and internal medicine by the healthcare industry. Well Med's coordinated care delivery system is designed to meet the individual healthcare needs of seniors.

Methodist Healthcare Ministries

Methodist Healthcare Ministries is a private, faith-based, not-for-profit organization dedicated to providing medical, dental and health-related human services to low-income families and the uninsured in South Texas. The mission of the organization is "Serving Humanity to Honor God" by improving the physical, mental and spiritual health of those least served in the Southwest Texas Conference area of The United Methodist Church. Methodist Healthcare Ministries is one-half owner of the Methodist Healthcare System - the largest healthcare system in South Texas.

Haven for Hope

Haven for Hope's mission is to offer hope and new beginnings by providing, coordinating, and delivering an efficient system of care for people experiencing homelessness in Bexar County.

I Care San Antonio

Founded in 1992 as a non-profit 501(c)(3), I Care San Antonio (ICSA) is a faith-based, medical organization managed primarily by volunteer doctors who provide dilated eye exams, refractions and glasses, and medical and surgical eye care to those who cannot afford it.

Baptist Health Foundation

The Baptist Health Foundation strives to become a philanthropic change-agent leader whose grants will make a difference in meeting the vast unmet healthcare needs in our region. We join with our distinguished funding partners in “Improving the Health of Our Community.”
