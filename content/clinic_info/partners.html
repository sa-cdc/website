Partnering Organizations

Teen Challenge of Texas

Teen Challenge of Texas welcomes individuals struggling with addiction, introduces them to God’s love, initiates the discipleship process, and trains them to demonstrate God’s love to others – magnifying God in the process.

SAMMinistries

SAMMinistries is an interfaith ministry whose mission is to help the homeless and those at risk of becoming homeless attain self-sufficiency by offering, with dignity and compassion, shelter, housing, and services. SAMMinistries also provides volunteers opportunities to be renewed and fulfilled as they serve.

Victory Outreach International

Victory Outreach is an international, church-oriented Christian ministry called to the task of evangelizing and discipling the hurting people of the world, with the message of hope and plan of Jesus Christ. This call involves a commitment to plant and develop churches, rehabilitation homes and training centers, in strategic cities of the world. Victory Outreach inspires and instills within people the desire to fulfill their potential in life with a sense of dignity, belonging, and destiny. Victory Outreach works cooperatively with others of mutual purpose in accomplishing the task before us.

Haven for Hope

Our mission is to offer a place of hope and new beginnings. We do this by providing, coordinating, and delivering and efficient system of care for people experiencing homelessness in Bexar County.

Catholic Charities

The mission of Catholic Charities is to provide for the needs of our community through selfless service under the sign of love. Committed to empowering individuals and families with the goal of fostering independence, we provide over 25 bilingual and culturally sensitive programs in 19 counties throughout the Archdiocese of San Antonio without regard to gender, race, creed, religion, disability, national origin, or socioeconomic status.

Christian Assistance Ministries

To share the love of Christ by providing immediate assistance and encouragement to people in crisis.

Family Violence Prevention Services, Inc.

The mission of Family Violence Prevention Services Inc. is to break the cycle of violence and to strengthen families, by providing the necessary tools for self-sufficiency through the delivery of emergency shelter, transitional housing, education, effective parenting education, and early intervention with children and youth.

National Vets Outreach

Our Mission is to establish and maintain a comprehensive community service agency with a diversified funding source that will serve the needs of veterans, their families, and other needy individuals of the community.

Salvation Army

The Salvation Army, an international movement, is an evangelical p class="partner"art of the universal Christian Church. Its message is based on the Bible. Its ministry is motivated by the love of God. Its mission is to preach the gospel of Jesus Christ and to meet human needs in His name without discrimination.

Alpha Home

The mission of Alpha Home is to provide spiritually based substance abuse treatment and support to women and their families to prepare them to become more productive members of society.

H.O.W. Foundation

The H.O.W. Foundation of San Antonio offers a unique approach for treating addiction.

WellMed

WellMed’s mission is to change the face of healthcare delivery for seniors. We have been recognized as a leader in preventive care and internal medicine by the healthcare industry, especially in Weslaco and McAllen, TX. Our Coordinated Care delivery system is designed to meet the individual healthcare needs of seniors.

I Care San Antonio

ICSA, through donations and volunteer time, strives to help people who might not otherwise be able to maintain this gift of sight.
