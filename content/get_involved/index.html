Get Involved.

Want to help the community? We need volunteers.

Why Get Involved

Volunteers are crucial to the success of our agency. In fact, each year SACDC relies upon its nearly 300 volunteers to bring help healing and hope to almost 8,000 patients. With so much need in our community, there is a need for nearly eery kind of skill!

""

Ways To Get Involved

Some examples of the kind of support we need

> Dental Staff: Dentists, Hygenists, RDAs, Sterilization, and Laboratory Technicians

> Administrative Volunteers: Office Receptionists, Record Maintenance, Database Entry

> Development and Marketing: Graphic Designers, Special Events Coordinators, Fundraising, Social Media Champions, and Business Outreach

""

Still Interested?

For those interested in volunteering, please fillout this application form and submit it by email to email@sacdc.org or bring it by the clinic so we can meet you in person. Questions? Call (210) 220-2301 for more information.
