Get Involved.

Want to help the community? We need volunteers.

Why Get Involved

Volunteers are crucial to the success of our agency. In fact, each year SACDC relies upon its nearly 300 volunteers to bring help, healing and hope to almost 8,000 patients. With so much need in our community, there is a need for nearly every kind of skill!

Ways To Get Involved

Some examples of the kind of support we need

> Dental Staff: Dentists, Hygienists, RDAs, sterilization and laboratory technicians

> Administrative Volunteers: Office receptionists, record maintenance, database entry

> Development and Marketing: Graphic designers, special events coordinators, fund-raising, social media champions, and business administration

Still Interested?

For those interested in volunteering, please fill-out this application form and submit it by email to info@sachristiandental.org or bring it by the clinic so we can meet you in person.
