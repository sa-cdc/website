Get Involved by Volunteering


As a nonprofit dental health provider, we rely heavily on the hard work of volunteers. If you would like to donate your time as a volunteer, we are need of help in the following areas:

  • Dentists
  • Hygienists
  • Dental Assistants
  • Sterilization Technicians
  • Laboratory Technicians
Non-Clinical Needs Include:
  • Office Receptionists
  • Telephone answering
  • Dental record maintenance
  • Database entry * Graphic Design
  • Special Events * Fundraising

To volunteer or to request more information you can:

  1. Complete the application form below. Once complete, you can email it, mail it, or bring it into our office.
  2. Give us a call at (210) 220-2301.
  3. Email us at email@sadc.com.
  4. Come by our office at the address in the footer of this page.
