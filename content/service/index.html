Get Involved by Making a Donation or Volunteering


As a nonprofit dental health provider, we rely heavily on individual monetary donations and the hard work of volunteers.

If you would like to make a monetary donation, please contact us via telephone at (210) 220-2300 or email at bigemf5@gmail.com.. Or, you can click on the link below to make a donation online. Your contributions are always appreciated!

If you would like to donate your time as a volunteer, we are need of help in the following areas:

  • Dentists
  • Hygienists
  • Dental Assistants
  • Sterilization Technicians
  • Laboratory Technicians
Non-Clinical Needs Include:
  • Office Receptionists
  • Telephone answering
  • Dental record maintenance
  • Database entry * Graphic Design
  • Special Events * Fundraising

To volunteer or to request more information you can:

  1. Complete the application form below. Once complete, you can email it, mail it, or bring it into our office.
  2. Give us a call at (210) 220-2301.
  3. Email us at bigemf5@gmail.com.
  4. Come by our office at the address in the footer of this page.
